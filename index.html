YouTube Video Uploader

Securely upload videos to your YouTube channel

About This App

This application allows you to upload videos directly to your YouTube channel using Google's official YouTube API. We prioritize your security and privacy - all uploads are processed directly between your browser and YouTube's servers.

How to Use:

  1. Sign in with your Google account
  2. Select your video file
  3. Add title and description
  4. Choose privacy setting
  5. Click upload

We only request the minimum required permissions to upload videos. Read our Privacy Policy for details.
